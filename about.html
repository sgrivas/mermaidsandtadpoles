Our Philosophy

We are proud to serve parents and individuals looking for high-quality, professional swimming education. Mermaids & Tadpoles is a premium learn-to-swim and performance-based swim school that teaches all skill levels ranging from the fundamentals of water safety to competitive instruction so that our swimmers love the water, are safe and swim for life. We are committed to making every lesson the start of a lifetime love of the water.

Meet Casey

Hi, my name is Casey Hurman. I’ve been married for almost 14 years and have two boys (ages 9 & 13). I now work full-time for the largest healthcare company in the world. A fun fact about me is that I enjoy the water more than being on land.

I’ve had experience swimming competitively for 12 years. Accolades include All-America in HS and College (DII). In 2011, I was a top 10 finisher at DII Nationals. I was a lifeguard for five years and a swim coach for two years. I’ve been teaching group and private swim lessons for almost 30 years.
